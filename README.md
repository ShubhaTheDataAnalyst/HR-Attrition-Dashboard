# 📊 HR Attrition Dashboard | SQL + Power BI

## 📖 Project Overview
This project analyzes employee data to identify key factors affecting attrition and helps organizations improve employee retention using data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify reasons behind employee attrition  
- Analyze trends across departments, roles, and demographics  
- Provide actionable recommendations for HR teams  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Data cleaning and analysis  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard and visualization  

---

## 📊 Dataset
- Source: Kaggle (HR Attrition Dataset)  
- Features include:
  - Age  
  - Department  
  - Job Role  
  - Salary  
  - Job Satisfaction  
  - Attrition  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing values  
- Standardized inconsistent data  
- Converted data types  

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Attrition rate calculation  
- Department-wise attrition  
- Job role analysis  
- Salary and satisfaction impact  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Employees, Attrition Rate, Total Attrition, Average Salary)  
- Attrition by Department and Job Role  
- Attrition By Age group
- Salary vs Attrition  
- Interactive Filters   

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is 16%, indicating moderate employee turnover.
- Sales Department shows highest Attrition rate that is greater than 20%,indicating potential issues in work load,performance   pressure.
- Attrition Rate by Age group is decresing over age.
- Lower salary groups have higher attrition, indicates no salary satisfaction.
- Attrition rate by gender in HR department shows male attrition is grater than Female Attrition.
- 40% Attrition rate in Sales representative job role, indicates low job satisfaction.

---

## 📌 Recommendations
- Improve employee engagement and satisfaction through regular feedback and support programm.
- Introduce performance-based incentives and rewards to motivate high-performing employees and enhance overall productivity.
- Focus on high-attrition departments by identifying specific issues and implementing targeted solutions.
- Provide career growth and development opportunities to retain skilled employees

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This dashboard helps HR teams understand attrition patterns and take proactive steps to improve employee retention.
